DNA Design Platform

Our DNA Design Platform focuses on next-generation plasmids for vaccines and gene therapy. Constructs are engineered for enhanced immunogenicity and durable expression. For gene therapy, we emphasize low-immunogenic backbones with reduced CpG content, optimized UTRs, and self-episomal replication in mitotic cells to enable persistent expression without genomic integration. Through sequence engineering and vector optimization, we deliver safe, efficient, and durable DNA platforms that bridge discovery and translational medicine.

DNA Delivery Platform

Our DNA Delivery Platform develops efficient, targeted, and safe systems for transporting genetic materials. We optimize delivery agents for improved DNA uptake and expression while maintaining low toxicity and minimal immunogenicity. By combining advanced formulation science with molecular engineering, we create versatile non-viral platforms for vaccines, gene therapy, and immunotherapy—delivering reliable performance across preclinical and translational studies. Vaxonyx is open to collaborations to co-develop and validate novel DNA delivery technologies that advance genetic medicines.

Vaxonyx builds future-ready bioinformatics platforms for neoantigen discovery, vaccine design, and translational immunology.
