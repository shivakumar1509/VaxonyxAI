Antibody Formats

From hybridoma generation to recombinant antibody engineering, we deliver antibodies tailored to your research or diagnostic applications.

  • Monoclonal antibodies (mouse, rabbit, rat)
  • Polyclonal antibodies (rabbit, goat, chicken)
  • Recombinant and humanized antibodies
  • Affinity and epitope mapping options

Expression Systems

We use mammalian (CHO, HEK293), insect (Sf9), and bacterial (E. coli) platforms to achieve high yield and functional quality.
