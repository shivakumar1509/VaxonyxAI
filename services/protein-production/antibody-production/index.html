Frequently asked

Which hosts do you recommend?
HEK293 for speed/transient screening; CHO for developability & scale. We can evaluate insect or E. coli per target constraints.
Can you deliver Fab/scFv/Fc-fusion?
Yes—Fab, F(ab')2, scFv, Fc-fusion, or custom isotypes/subclasses are supported.
What QC is included?
Standard: SDS-PAGE, WB (as needed), concentration, endotoxin; optional: HPLC-SEC, MS, binding assays.

Send target, isotype/format, yield, and buffer preferences. We’ll scope options and a rapid quote.
