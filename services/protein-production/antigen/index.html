Comprehensive Antigen Expertise

At Vaxonyx, we specialize in the design, expression, and purification of custom antigens tailored to meet the needs of research, diagnostics, and vaccine development. Our expertise covers both recombinant and native antigen production, enabling us to deliver biologically relevant and application-ready materials with superior quality and consistency.

Recombinant Antigens

We develop high-purity recombinant proteins using bacterial, mammalian, and insect cell expression systems. Each antigen is engineered for optimal yield, solubility, and native folding, ensuring performance across immunoassays, antibody generation, and functional studies. Sequence verification, purification optimization, and quality control (SDS-PAGE, Western blot, ELISA) are included in every project.

Native Antigens

Our team is experienced in isolating and purifying native antigens from a broad range of biological sources. These naturally derived proteins preserve authentic conformational epitopes, making them ideal for diagnostic assays and vaccine research.

Veterinary Pathogens
