Protein Production Services

Excelling in both protein expression and purification, we offer tailored solutions for protein production using mammalian, insect, yeast, and E. coli expression platforms—designed to meet the diverse and constantly evolving needs of our clients.

Key Features

  • High-Titer Expression: Optimized systems ensure robust protein yields.
  • Rapid Turnaround: Typical delivery in 4–6 weeks with streamlined operations.
  • Economical Solutions: High titer reduces culture volume and time for cost-effective outcomes.

Process of Protein Production

Protein production—including expression and purification—is a recombinant DNA–enabled workflow that generates large quantities of a specific protein of interest for research, diagnostics, therapeutics, and industrial use.

Protein Expression

We support multiple systems to match your project’s needs:

Mammalian Expression

HEK293 and CHO suspension cultures for large-scale recombinant proteins and antibodies (intracellular, secreted, or membrane). We support transient transfection, BacMam transduction, and stable cell line development for high yield and reproducibility.

Insect Expression

Baculovirus-mediated expression in insect cells enables high yields and fast turnaround with near-mammalian post-translational modifications once virus stocks are prepared.

Bacterial (E. coli) Expression

Reliable, reproducible production using diverse E. coli strains at bioreactor scale, up to 40 L per batch.

Yeast Expression

High-yield expression with mammalian-like modifications for proteins that are difficult in bacteria, leveraging a secretory system similar to mammalian cells and straightforward genetic manipulation.

Protein Purification

We isolate target proteins from complex mixtures by leveraging size, charge, and hydrophobicity—delivering proteins at >90% purity for structural and functional studies.
