Cell Line Development Services

We specialize in developing stable and transient, high-performing mammalian cell lines tailored to your project needs. From early-stage clone generation to production-ready master cell banks, we prioritize speed, reliability, and regulatory compliance.

Why Choose Us?

  • Custom Solutions: CHO, HEK293, or client-specified systems.
  • Rapid Timelines: Streamlined workflows accelerate clone selection.
  • High Yield & Stability: Optimized vectors and selection strategies for long-term expression.
  • Regulatory Ready: GLP/GMP-aligned documentation and quality control.

Our Capabilities

Vector Design & Transfection

  • Codon optimization and vector engineering.
  • Transient expression and stable integration strategies.

Clone Selection & Screening

  • Automated single-cell cloning.
  • High-throughput screening for productivity and quality.

Process Development

  • Optimization for titer, purity, and reproducibility.

Cell Banking & Characterization

  • Creation of Master and Working Cell Banks.
  • Identity, stability, and biosafety testing.
  • Cell culture services.

Applications

  • Monoclonal antibody production
  • Recombinant protein therapeutics
  • Vaccine development
  • Gene therapy vectors

Quality You Can Trust

Our team combines molecular biology expertise with state-of-the-art technology to deliver stable, high-yielding cell lines designed to accelerate your research, diagnostics, or therapeutic programs.

Stable Cell Line Workflow (illustrative timeline)

  1. Gene synthesis & plasmid construction (2–3 weeks)
  2. Transfection & analysis (4–5 weeks)
  3. Cloning (6–8 weeks)
  4. Verification & production (3–5 weeks)
  5. Delivery with COA (2 weeks)
