Biochemical Assay Services

Accurate. Reliable. Data-Driven. At Vaxonyx LLC, we specialize in the design and execution of biochemical assays to support discovery, translational, and therapeutic research. Our team integrates molecular biology, immunology, and analytical expertise to deliver high-quality, reproducible results.

Assay Development & Optimization

  • Custom assay design based on target or pathway of interest
  • Optimization for sensitivity, specificity, and reproducibility
  • Statistical and analytical report generation
  • Data-driven insights to guide next-step decisions

Enzymatic & Binding Assays

  • Enzyme kinetics (Michaelis–Menten, IC₅₀, EC₅₀)
  • Protein–protein and protein–ligand interaction studies (ELISA, SPR, BLI)
  • Inhibition and cofactor characterization
  • Fluorescence, luminescence, and absorbance-based assays

Cell-Based Functional Assays

  • Cytotoxicity and viability testing (MTT, LDH, ATP-based)
  • Reporter gene and signal transduction assays
  • Mitochondrial function and oxidative stress studies
  • Cytokine and chemokine profiling (ELISA, multiplex formats)

Molecular & Analytical Methods

  • Quantitative PCR and qRT-PCR assays
  • Protein and peptide quantification (BCA, Bradford, Lowry)
  • Western blotting and densitometry analysis
  • Spectrophotometric and image-based measurements

Data Analysis & Interpretation

  • Statistical modeling and curve fitting (GraphPad Prism, SigmaPlot, R, Python)
  • Dose–response and comparative analysis
  • Custom data visualization and report generation

Deliverables

  • Comprehensive reports with analyzed data and summary tables
  • Graphical presentation of key findings
  • Optional integration with AI-driven data analytics

Vaxonyx builds future-ready bioinformatics platforms for neoantigen discovery, vaccine design, and translational immunology.
