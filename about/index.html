About Vaxonyx

Designing tools to unlock next-generation vaccine design and delivery.

Mission

At Vaxonyx, we are building the foundation for a new era in vaccine and immunotherapy innovation. Our mission is to harness the full potential of genomics, bioinformatics, and molecular design to accelerate discoveries that directly improve human and animal health.

Through advanced platforms in bulk RNA-seq, single-cell RNA-seq, variant calling, HLA typing, and neoantigen discovery, we turn complex datasets into clear, actionable insights that drive translational breakthroughs.

We bridge computation and biology with precision. Every workflow—from immune profiling to vaccine target validation— is built for reproducibility, scalability, and clinical relevance. Our mission is not just to analyze data, but to enable discovery—helping partners move from sequence to solution faster and more accurately than ever before.

Vision

Over the next decade, Vaxonyx will stand at the forefront of a global transformation in how vaccines and immunotherapies are designed. We envision a future where therapeutic decisions are data-informed, antigens are computationally optimized before synthesis, and vaccines are tailored to genetic and immunologic profiles.

Our vision is to make computational immunology the engine of modern medicine—transforming raw genomic information into predictive models that guide vaccine design, immune-response forecasting, and personalized therapeutic development.

By integrating AI-driven analytics, high-throughput sequencing, and molecular engineering into one cohesive ecosystem, we will expand our technologies, collaborations, and impact until precision vaccine design becomes the new standard of care.

Team

We are scientists, engineers, and builders advancing computational immunology and translational biology.
